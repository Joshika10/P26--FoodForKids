CHOCOLATE SMOOTIE  RECIPE

INGREDIANTS -

2 Frozen Bananas, 1/4 cup of coconut milk, 3 dates (seedless), 1 tablespoon almonds or peanut butter, 1 tablespoon cocao powder.

PRE-PREPARATION -

1. Take the banana, peel it, slice it, cut it into small pieces and freeze it for     about 6 hours.
2. prepare the coconut milk at home.
3. prepare the peanut butter at home.

METHOD -
1. Place all the ingrediants ito a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Pour into bowls and and top with fresh seasonal fruits or nut with your     choice.
3.Enjoy eating a healthy chocolate smothie bowl.
